Islamabad is known for its parks and forests, including the Margalla Hills National Park and the Shakarparian. Islamabad is the world's second most beautiful capital, designed uniquely and made eco-friendly.This city has a lot to offer, an eye-catching view, a peaceful environment, developed infrastructure, clean roads, and friendly people.It is situated in the center of a series of beautiful mountains surrounded by Margallah Hills and full of greenery. Because of the huge plantations, rain is very often in the city. The pouring of rain from glorious mountain enhance the beauty and make the city mesmerizingly beautiful.
